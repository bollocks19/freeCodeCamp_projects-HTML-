Ingredients

  • Low-calorie tortilla
  • light mozarella
  • ham
  • homemade tomato sauce
  • (optional) dried oregano

Instructions

  1. Pre-heat the oven at 200 celsius degrees
  2. First you take a tortilla and put it onto a baking sheet
  3. After you spread the sauce right to the edges (if you do it like this your base will not burn)
  4. Spread your mozarella as much as you want
  5. Slice your ham into squares
  6. Spread the ham evenly
  7. Put it in the oven for aprox. 10 minutes or until the cheese has melted
